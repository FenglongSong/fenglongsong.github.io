Modeling, simulation and motor selection for UR10 robot arm

Course: Introduction to Robotics, Advisor: Prof. Ye Ding

Responsible for calculating forward and inverse kinematics based on D-H coordinates method in MATLAB and simulation with SimMechanics Multibody Library in Simulink

Intro: We built mathematics model for both forward and inverse kinematics of UR10 manipulator based on D-H coordinates and exponential coordinates method, Firstly we modeled the arm using D-H coordinates in MATLAB . Secondly we calculated built the dynamics model for UR10 manipulator based on Newton-Euler method ,wrote it into a MATLAB function and then compare the result with Robotics Toolbox. Thirdly, we used Simulink, Adams as well as ROS MoveIt to simulate the motion of robot. Finally, we selected motors and reducers for each joint based on our calculating results.
